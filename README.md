# Soup-Dino-Game
made for Fallout! [organised by Hackclub, 1st July - 7th July 2026]
created by onlyth3best, blowupthenoobs, & trpalt

Ever wanted to play the offline dinasour game in real life? Well, now's your chance!
Our magnificent contraption works with vibration sensors/accelerometers & XIAO RP2040s in order to detect movement and coordinate between microcontrollers.
